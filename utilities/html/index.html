Complex Number Calculator Help

Introduction

The Complex Number Calculator allows you to perform calculations with complex numbers. A complex number is a number that can be expressed in the form a + bi, where a and b are real numbers, and i is the imaginary unit, which is equal to the square root of -1.

With the calculator, you can perform addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division with complex numbers, as well as exponents, logarithms and other conversions/operations.
